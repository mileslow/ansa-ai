## Pipeline use note

These documents should be classified as **instruction/decision sources**, not treated as official plan-benefit evidence. When they conflict with an SBC, SPD, certificate, policy, or signed carrier proposal, the pipeline should preserve both claims, apply the configured source-precedence rules, and ask for clarification where the conflict affects booklet accuracy. A completed and signed election/selection form may be authoritative for what the employer chose, while official plan documents remain authoritative for the actual benefit terms.
