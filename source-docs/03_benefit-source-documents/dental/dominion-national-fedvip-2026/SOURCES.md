## Inclusion boundary

The official OPM brochure governs Dominion National's regional FEDVIP dental EPO
and includes High and Standard options, each with Self Only, Self Plus One, and
Self and Family enrollment types. It is retained as one combined source rather
than six artificial copies. Other FEDVIP carriers and Dominion products outside
this OPM contract are separate offerings and are excluded.
