## Inclusion boundary

The certificate is the governing New York State Dental Plan document updated for
the October 2024 Anthem administrator transition. It describes one plan with
participating- and non-participating-provider benefit levels. Covered bargaining
groups and enrollment classes are eligibility categories, not richer or leaner
plan tiers. Other NYSHIP group-specific dental documents are therefore not
siblings of this offering and are excluded.
