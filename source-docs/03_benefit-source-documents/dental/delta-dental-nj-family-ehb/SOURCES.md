## Inclusion boundary

Delta Dental's current first-party ACA-qualified plan page presents Basic Family
I, Basic Family II, and Enhanced Family III as the three levels in this family
EHB offering. All three full policies are included. Separate pediatric-only
plans, non-ACA individual plans, small-group products, and the newer commercial
Progressive/Premium/Classic portfolio are different product families and are
excluded.

The policy form identifiers end in SPEC 4.22. They are included because Delta
Dental still serves these exact official policy PDFs and its current portfolio
materials continue to identify the three plan levels; they should not be
misrepresented as documents first authored in 2026.
