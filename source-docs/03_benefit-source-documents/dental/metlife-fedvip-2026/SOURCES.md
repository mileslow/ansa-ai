## Inclusion boundary

The OPM brochure is the official statement of benefits for the MetLife Federal
Dental Plan and contains both High and Standard options. Each option includes
Self Only, Self Plus One, and Self and Family enrollment types. Because OPM
publishes all six combinations in one governing brochure, the file is retained
once under high-and-standard-options instead of being duplicated six times.
Other FEDVIP carriers are separate offerings and are excluded.
