## Inclusion boundary

CalHR identifies Western Dental as one prepaid DHMO option for 2026. Its
procedure-level member copayments live in a separate Standard Plan Schedule of
Benefits, so the EOC and schedule are retained together under standard-dhmo.
CalHR's Delta Dental, DeltaCare, MetLife, and Premier Access choices are separate
carrier offerings, not Western Dental tiers. Marketing and provider-finder
flyers are excluded because they do not add a separate benefit option.

CalHR states that Western Dental ends December 31, 2026; that program change does
not alter the scope of this 2026 source set.
