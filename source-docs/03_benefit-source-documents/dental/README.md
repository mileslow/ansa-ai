# Dental plan document examples

Five public, real dental-benefit documents selected to exercise materially different extraction paths. The set spans nationwide and regional federal plans, a state public-employer plan, a California dental HMO, and an individual/family policy with pediatric essential-health-benefit coverage. These are source examples, not recommendations or current eligibility determinations.

Retrieval date for every file: **2026-07-17**.

## 1. The MetLife Federal Dental Plan (2026)

## 2. Dominion National FEDVIP Dental Plan (2026)

- **Organization:** Dominion Dental Services, Inc. (Dominion National); authorized for distribution by OPM
- **Jurisdiction / market:** Regional federal market covering the District of Columbia, Delaware, Maryland, Pennsylvania, parts of Virginia, and parts of New Jersey

## 4. Western Dental Evidence of Coverage (2026)

- **Organization:** Western Dental Services, Inc.; published through the California Department of Human Resources (CalHR)
- **Jurisdiction / market:** California state-sponsored employee group dental coverage
- **Plan design:** Dental HMO / prepaid managed-care plan using participating dental facilities, primary providers, referrals, and scheduled member copayments

## Verification method

Each download was required to resolve as a real PDF rather than an HTML or error response. The final files were checked with `file` and `pdfinfo`, sampled with `pdftotext`, rendered on page 1 with `pdftoppm`, and visually inspected. All five page-one renders were readable, complete, and free of obvious clipping, overlap, missing-font blocks, or corruption.
