# University of Rochester 2026 employee medical options

Retrieved and verified on 2026-07-17.

## Offering scope

The University of Rochester's official employee health-care page states that it offers two medical plans for 2026, both administered through Excellus BlueCross BlueShield:

- YOUR PPO Plan
- YOUR HSA-Eligible Plan

This folder contains the official 2026 Summary of Benefits and Coverage for each option.

### Inclusion and exclusion logic

- Included both medical options named by the University for the 2026 plan year.
- Did not create separate PDFs for Tier 1 Accountable Health Partners, Tier 2 Excellus National Network, or Tier 3 out-of-network. These are provider-network cost levels inside each plan, and both SBCs describe their applicable levels in one document.
- Did not create separate files for employee-only and family coverage because they are coverage levels within the same plan and appear together in each SBC.
- Excluded dental, vision, FSA/HSA accounts, and standalone prescription support material because they are not additional medical plan options. Pharmacy benefits embedded in each medical plan remain described in its SBC.
