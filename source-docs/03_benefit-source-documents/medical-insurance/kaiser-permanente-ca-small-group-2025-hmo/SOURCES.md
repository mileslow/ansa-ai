# Kaiser Permanente California small-group HMO options (2025)

Retrieved: 2026-07-17

## Scope

The anchor document, `bronze-60-hdhp-hmo-6650-0-pcp.pdf`, appears in Kaiser Permanente's official archive under **2025 plans offered by Kaiser Permanente**. This folder therefore contains every English base HMO design in that same published 2025 California small-group lineup, across Bronze 60, Silver 70, Gold 80, and Platinum 90. HDHP and HRA HMO designs are retained because Kaiser lists them as HMO options in the same offering.

Official lineup index: [Kaiser Permanente small-groups SBC archive](https://healthy.kaiserpermanente.org/southern-california/support/forms/health-plans/small-groups-summary-benefits-coverage/archive)

Excluded on purpose:

- The separate **plans offered by Covered California** section, because the anchor PDF is from the Kaiser Permanente-offered section.
- `w INF` copies, because these are parallel infertility-benefit rider variants rather than additional metal tiers; the base designs are retained here.
- PPO designs, because they are a different network/product family from the anchor HMO.
- Spanish, Chinese, and Vietnamese translations, because they duplicate the English plan designs.
- CalChoice plans, individual/family plans, other plan years, and Evidence of Coverage files, because they belong to different offerings, years, or document types.
