## Inclusion boundary

The single CalHR/VSP evidence of coverage governs Basic Plan 30052011 and
Premier Plan 30034581. Basic is the state-paid base benefit and Premier is the
employee-elected upgrade. Because both real options are defined and compared in
one governing EOC, the document remains combined under basic-and-premier-options
instead of being split or duplicated. No third CalHR VSP option is identified by
the official source.
