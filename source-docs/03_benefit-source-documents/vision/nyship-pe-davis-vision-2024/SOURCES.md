## Inclusion boundary

The plan book identifies one Standard vision plan for participating-employer
employees and their enrolled dependents. Individual/family enrollment and
age-based service frequencies are coverage categories within that plan, not
separate benefit tiers. New York publishes different books for other bargaining
units and employee groups, but those are separate employer-group configurations,
not siblings of this PE offering, so they are excluded.
