## Inclusion boundary

EyeMed's official comparison presents Bright, Bold, and Healthy side by side.
Those are the three meaningful levels in this source, so the comparison is
retained once under bright-bold-healthy-options rather than copied into three
files. Employer-group and state-specific EyeMed certificates are separate
offerings and are excluded.

The PDF carries older production/form dates and is a summary rather than a
state-specific policy. It is retained because EyeMed's current first-party
individual page still links to it; it must not be represented as a 2026-authored
policy or used without checking state availability and controlling terms.
