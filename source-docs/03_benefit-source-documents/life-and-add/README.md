## 01 - Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance Handbook

- **Title:** Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance (FEGLI) Program Handbook
- **Organization:** U.S. Office of Personnel Management; FEGLI coverage is contracted through Metropolitan Life Insurance Company
- **Jurisdiction/market:** United States federal and postal employees, annuitants, and compensationers
- **Plan/program design:** Eligible employees are generally enrolled automatically in Basic coverage unless they waive it. Basic is based on annual basic pay rounded up to the next $1,000 plus $2,000; the federal government pays one-third of the Basic premium for most federal employees. Employee-paid Optional coverage includes Option A ($10,000), Option B (one to five multiples of pay), and Option C family coverage (one to five multiples for spouse and eligible children). Basic and Option A include employee AD&D. The handbook also explains retiree/post-65 reduction choices.
- **Document subtype:** Detailed federal life-insurance program handbook
- **Why distinct:** It is a nationwide statutory public program rather than a single-employer carrier certificate, and it combines Basic, supplemental, family/dependent, AD&D, claims, assignment, living-benefit, and retirement rules in one large reference.
- **Source webpage:** https://www.opm.gov/healthcare-insurance/life-insurance/reference-materials/publications-forms/fegli-handbook/

## 02 - State of Minnesota Group Term Life Certificate

- **Title:** Group Term Life Certificate of Insurance
- **Organization:** State of Minnesota; issued by Minnesota Life Insurance Company, a Securian company
- **Jurisdiction/market:** Minnesota state employees and other populations eligible under Minnesota Statutes chapter 43A
- **Plan/program design:** Effective January 1, 2025. Basic benefits vary by bargaining unit and employee class, with employer-paid Basic for Type A employees and contributory coverage for additional and dependent benefits. Additional employee life and spouse life are available in $5,000 increments up to $500,000; child life is $10,000. AD&D generally matches insured Basic/additional employee life and insured spouse life. Initial guaranteed issue includes all Basic life, employee additional life up to two times earnings, and $10,000 each for spouse and child coverage.
- **Document subtype:** Full group term-life certificate with specifications, benefit schedules, and supplements
- **Why distinct:** Demonstrates one state program with many bargaining-unit benefit tables, employee and dependent coverages, guaranteed-issue thresholds, post-retirement death benefits, matching AD&D, and transitions between employee and spouse status.

## 03 - City of Seattle Voluntary AD&D Certificate

- **Title:** Group Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) Certificate of Insurance
- **Organization:** City of Seattle; issued by Securian Life Insurance Company
- **Jurisdiction/market:** Washington; municipal employees and eligible dependents
- **Plan/program design:** Effective January 1, 2021. Employee AD&D is voluntary/contributory and elected in $25,000 increments up to $500,000. Under family coverage, spouse/domestic-partner benefits equal 50% of the employee amount when children are covered or 60% without children; each child receives 15% when a spouse/partner is covered or 20% without one, subject to stated spouse and child maxima.
