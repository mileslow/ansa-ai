# Completed employer-guide style references

This folder contains five public, real employee-benefits guides selected as structural references for booklet generation. Publicly downloadable, truly blank/customizable master templates are scarce; these files are therefore **model guides, not blank templates**. Together they cover municipal, county, higher-education, healthcare-system, and public-safety audiences, with materially different information architectures and visual formats.

All examples were retrieved on **2026-07-17** from official organization websites.

## Rights and reuse caution

Use these files to study section order, modularity, navigation, comparison-table patterns, and the kinds of information a benefits booklet may contain. Do **not** copy organization-specific facts, rates, contacts, legal language, logos, photography, plan terms, or branded design elements into another employer's booklet. These are published examples, not licensed design templates. The source plan documents and employer instructions must remain authoritative for generated content.
